Privacy Policy for Todolyfy

Last Updated: July 13, 2025

Welcome to Todolyfy! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website and Chrome Extension. Our goal is to be as transparent as possible about the data we use to provide and improve our service.

The Core Principle

Our core principle is to collect the absolute minimum amount of information necessary to make our application functional and to improve the user experience.

What Information We Collect

When you use the Todolyfy application, we collect two types of data:

  1. Task Content: The text of the to-do list items that you enter.
  2. Anonymous Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated data about how users interact with our website.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or any other personal data that is not explicitly provided by you.

How We Use Your Information

The data you provide is used for two specific purposes:

  • To Generate AI-Powered Subtasks: The text of your task is sent securely to our backend service, which uses the Google Gemini AI API to analyze the task. The AI then generates a relevant list of subtasks, which is sent back to you. This text is used "in-the-moment" to fulfill this request and is not stored or logged on our servers.
  • To Improve Our Service: We use the anonymous data from Google Analytics to understand how our users engage with Todolyfy. This helps us identify popular features, find areas for improvement, and make the application better for everyone.

Google Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to help us understand user behavior. Google Analytics uses "cookies" to collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior information in an anonymous form. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website (including a truncated IP address) is transmitted to Google.

This information is then used to evaluate visitors' use of the website and to compile statistical reports on website activity for Todolyfy. We will never use the statistical analytics tool to track or to collect any Personally Identifiable Information.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

Data Storage (Chrome Extension)

For our Chrome Extension, all of your created tasks and subtasks are stored locally on your own device using Chrome's built-in storage.sync API. This means your to-do list is private to you and is not stored on our servers.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties, with the sole exception of the services mentioned above (Google Gemini AI and Google Analytics), which are essential for the functionality and improvement of the extension.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us at info@todolyfy.com.
